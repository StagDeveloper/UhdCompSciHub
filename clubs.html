CS Clubs at UHD

Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)

Our mission is to empower students and help them find their path in Computer Science, Data Science or MIS. We do so by running a variety of student-led programming workshops, events, coding competitions, and more.

Computing Alliance of Hispanic-Serving Institutions (CAHSI)

Our mission is to grow and sustain a networked community committed to recruiting, retaining and accelerating the progress of Hispanics in computing at UHD. CAHSI hosts varieties of workshops and events to gain professional skills for the STEM industry.
