CS4303-Programming Language Concept

Class information

She is a great professor, she is well organized with the schedule of the courework. In this class, you will learn about imperative, functional, logic and object-oriented paradigms. Her lectures consist of slides and lab work which can also be reviewed from blackboard. She gives clear instructions on how to complete assignments and generally replies within a day. Her exams are a combination of multiple choice questions and a few programming questions. You will do really well if you follow her lectures and slides, she also posts exam reviews.
